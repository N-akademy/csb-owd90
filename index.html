About the Kingdom

The king of the region of Colchis (present-day Georgia), 1375 kilometers from Iolcos. He is the Guardian of the Golden Fleece and will challenge Jason to retrieve the Fleece. The beautiful and young daughter of King Aeetes promised to Jason (with the Golden Fleece).
